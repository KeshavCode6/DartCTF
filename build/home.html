PresentingDart CTF

A CTF game crafted to engage participants in a variety of challenges. Whether you're a cybersecurity enthusiast or complete begginner, Dart CTF attempts to cater to all skill levels, providing an inclusive and challenging environment for everyone. This project was developed for the 2024 Lanier Regional Technology Competition by Keshav Shah and Shaurya Kumar (more information available on the about page). Whether you're a coding maestro or a complete beginner, Dart CTF is your chance to showcase your skills and improve your cybersecurity knowledge. Are you ready to face the challenge? Sign up today for free with just your Google account!

Learn Cybersecurity

Dive into the fundamental concepts of cybersecurity, such as steganography, website manipulation, cryptography, and more. Whether you're a beginner or looking to expand your knowledge, our platform provides the resources you need to start your journey into the exciting world of cybersecurity.

Learn Programming

Explore the basics of Python programming, including loops, logic, and more. Our platform caters to programmers of all levels, with challenges specifically designed for beginners. Whether you're just starting out or looking to sharpen your skills, join us to unlock the power of programming.

Have Fun and Compete

Dart CTF values learning through enjoyable exploration and engagement. Our challenges are meticulously crafted to strike a balance between fun and accomplishment, ensuring maximum fun and meaningful learning experiences! Dart CTF can be as competitive as you want, catering to those seeking a challenge.
