PresentingDart CTF

A CTF game crafted to engage participants in a variety of challenges. Whether you're a cybersecurity enthusiast or complete beginner, DartCTF attempts to cater to all skill levels, providing an inclusive and challenging environment for everyone. This project was developed for the 2024 Lanier Regional Technology Competition and the GASTC by Keshav Shah and Shaurya Kumar (more information available on the about page). We are currently competing at a state level. Whether you're a cybersecurity maestro or a complete beginner, DartCTF is your chance to showcase your skills and improve your cybersecurity knowledge. Are you ready to face the challenge? Sign up today for free with just your Google account! Start now, today!

Cybersecurity

Check your fundamental concepts of cybersecurity, such as steganography, website manipulation, cryptography, and more. Whether you're a beginner or looking to expand your knowledge, our platform provides the resources you need to start your journey into the exciting world of cybersecurity.

Programming

Test your basics of programming, including loops, logic, and more. Our platform caters to programmers of all levels, with challenges specifically designed for beginners. Whether you're just starting out or looking to sharpen your skills, join us to unlock the power of programming.

Have Fun and Compete

We value learning through enjoyable exploration and engagement. Our challenges are meticulously crafted to strike a balance between fun and accomplishment, ensuring maximum fun and meaningful learning experiences! DartCTF can be as competitive as you want, catering to those seeking a challenge.
