Presenting DART CTF!

A CTF game crafted to engage participants in a variety of challenges. Whether you're a cybersecurity enthusiast or complete begginner, DART CTF attempts to cater to all skill levels, providing an inclusive and challenging environment for everyone. This project was developed for the Lanier Tech Fair 2024 by Keshav Shah and Shaurya Kumar (more information available on the Contact page). Whether you're a coding maestro or a complete begginner, DART CTF is your chance to showcase your skills, and improve your cybersecurity knowledge. Are you ready to face the challenge? Signup today for FREE with just your google account!

Programming Challenges

Dive into a world of coding where your ability to solve complex problems and write efficient code will be put to the test. From algorithmic puzzles to code-breaking scenarios, expect a diverse range of programming challenges that will ignite your logical thinking.

Cybersecurity Fun!

Immerse yourself in the fascinating realm of cybersecurity with challenges ranging from cryptography to network exploitation. Uncover vulnerabilities, decrypt messages, and thwart cyber threats as you navigate through a series of real-world scenarios.

Learning Opportunities: Beyond the thrill of competition, the Lanier Tech Fair CTF provides a unique learning experience. Participants will gain valuable insights into the latest trends in programming and cybersecurity, enhancing their skills in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.
